A mouse is a small rodent with a pointed snout, small rounded ears and a body-length scaly tail. Researchers have confirmed that mice have a range of facial expressions when they used machine vision to spot familiar human emotions like pleasure, disgust, nausea, pain, and fear. While communicating with each other, mice make ultrasonic sounds as well as regular ones.
